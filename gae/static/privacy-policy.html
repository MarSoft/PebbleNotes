PebNotes — GoogleTasks for Pebble

Privacy Policy

This app is just a client for Google Tasks. It does have a server which is used as an authentication proxy for Google auth. Our server does not store any of your data.

We do not store any of your personal data. All processing happens on your phone and your Pebble device.

Authentication credentials are only stored in the Pebble app local storage, and you can always invalidate your authentication credentials using the "Logout" button in app settings.

You can also see the source code of this app on GitHub.
