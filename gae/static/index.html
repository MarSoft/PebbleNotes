GoogleTasks for Pebble (ex. PebbleNotes)

This is a Google Tasks client for Pebble smartwatch.

You may discuss it here.

Available in Rebble AppStore.

Authentication

You can login with your Google account and obtain passcode. Then enter obtained passcode to application's settings.

Pebble is fading away, but is still alive. This application is one of the examples. You can donate to support it.

Privacy Policy

We do not store any of your personal data. All processing happens on your phone and your Pebble device. Our server is used only to proxy authentication data and does not store anything.
